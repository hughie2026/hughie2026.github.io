About

I am an Associate Professor at the University of Tourism, Macao. My research focuses on global health, health systems and governance, AI-oriented decision science, data analytics, causal inference, medical tourism, and travel medicine. I am also actively engaged in interdisciplinary teaching and applied research in public health and health data science.
