About Me

I am an Associate Professor at the Macao University of Tourism, working at the intersection of global health, data science, and travel medicine. My research applies decision science, causal inference, and AI-oriented analytics to public health problems, with a particular focus on health systems, social determinants of health, and travel epidemiology.

I currently serve as Executive Director of the Hong Kong Public Health Technology Research Center, and previously held appointments as Discipline Leader at Guangzhou College of Commerce and Postdoctoral Research Fellow at Macau University of Science and Technology.

Research Interests

  • Global Health

    Social factors, health systems, and governance.

  • Decision Science & AI

    Data analytics and causal inference for public health.

  • Travel Medicine

    Medical tourism, travel epidemiology, and disease mobility.
