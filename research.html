Research

Research Themes

1. Global Health: Social Factors, Health Systems, and Governance

Examining how social determinants and policy frameworks shape population health outcomes, with empirical work on mental health, severe mental disorders, and health behaviors in Greater Bay Area populations.

2. Decision Science, Data Analytics, and Causal Inference (AI-Oriented)

Developing interpretable machine-learning systems for clinical decision support, including exercise prescription for cardio-oncology preventive care and predictive analytics for pediatric mental health.

3. Medical Tourism, Travel Epidemiology, and Travel Medicine

Studying disease mobility, surveillance, and governance in a globalized world, including modeling the geographical spread of infectious diseases and building personalized travel health decision-support systems.
